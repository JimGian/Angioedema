Recognise the danger

Sudden swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat can be life-threatening. If breathing is affected, call emergency services immediately.

💊

It is treatable

Most forms of angioedema respond well to medication. Early diagnosis and identifying the trigger are key to preventing recurrence.

🔬

It can be identified

Blood tests, allergy panels, and a detailed medical history help doctors confirm the type and underlying cause of angioedema.
