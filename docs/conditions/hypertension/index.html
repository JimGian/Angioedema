Hypertension

December 8, 2025

title: “Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)” draft: false

Hypertension is a long-term medical condition in which the blood pressure in the arteries is persistently elevated.

Symptoms

  • Often no symptoms (it can be “silent”)
  • Headaches
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nosebleeds (less common)

Risks

  • Heart attack
  • Stroke
  • Kidney disease

When to see a doctor

If you have high blood pressure readings or symptoms such as chest pain, shortness of breath, or severe headache, contact a healthcare professional.
