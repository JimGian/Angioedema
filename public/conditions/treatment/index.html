Treatment

Treatment depends on the cause and how serious the swelling is.

🚑 Emergency care
  • Epinephrine for allergic reactions
  • Airway support if breathing is blocked
  • Hospital observation for severe swelling
  • Rapid treatment for throat or tongue involvement
💊 Common treatments
  • Antihistamines for allergy-related swelling
  • Steroids for inflammation
  • C1 inhibitor medicines for hereditary angioedema
  • Avoiding known triggers when possible
🧘 Long-term management
  • Keep a symptom diary
  • Carry emergency medication if prescribed
  • Know your triggers and avoid them
  • Stay in contact with your healthcare provider

Note

If you have questions about your care, talk with a doctor or specialist.
